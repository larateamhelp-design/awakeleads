FAQ

Common Questions

What kind of businesses does this work for?
Any business with a lead list and a sales process — HVAC, roofing, insurance, solar, mortgage, legal, home services. If your customers need to talk to someone before buying, this works.
How old can the leads be?
We've seen results from leads over a year old. As long as they gave you their phone number at some point, they're worth texting. Most businesses are sitting on untouched gold.
What does it cost?
Performance-based only — you pay per confirmed booked appointment. Zero upfront. If we don't book calls, you don't pay. Pricing is discussed on the strategy call based on your industry and deal size.
How long does setup take?
3–5 business days from receiving your lead list. First replies typically come in within 24–48 hours of launch.
Is this legal? Can you text old leads?
Yes — leads who previously submitted their information to your business can be re-contacted. We handle A2P registration, compliance, and opt-out language in every message.
