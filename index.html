FAQ

Common Questions

What kind of businesses does this work for?
Any business with a sales team and a database of leads that didn't convert. HVAC, solar, roofing, insurance, mortgage, legal, real estate, home renovation — if people have to sell it over the phone or in person, this works.
How old can the leads be?
We've successfully reactivated leads that are 2+ years old. As long as they submitted their info at some point, they're worth texting. Most businesses are sitting on gold they've completely written off.
What does it cost?
We work on a performance basis — you pay per confirmed booked appointment. Zero upfront. If we don't book calls, you don't pay. We discuss specific pricing on the demo call based on your industry and average deal size.
How long does setup take?
Usually 3–5 business days from receiving your lead list to your AI being live and texting contacts. The first replies typically come in within 24–48 hours of launch.
Is this compliant? Can you text old leads?
Yes — leads who previously submitted their information to your business can be re-contacted. Our AI also includes proper opt-out language in every message. We handle A2P registration and compliance setup.
What if my leads are really old or low quality?
That's exactly the point. We work with leads others have written off. If you have a list of 1,000 leads and even 5% book calls, that's 50 new opportunities from a database you weren't using at all.
